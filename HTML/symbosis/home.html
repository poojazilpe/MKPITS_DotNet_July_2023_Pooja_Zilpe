Symbiosis International University)

Symbiosis School of Planning Architecture and Design, also known as SSPAD is a constituent institute of Symbiosis International Deemed to be University,

(SIU) Nagpur campus, which was established in 1995. SIU has six campuses in Pune, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Noida, Nashik and Nagpur. The University is recognised by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and

is re-accredited with an “A” grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
