About us

Although famous for Management courses offered by it, Symbiosis International University (SIU) also offers programmes in the field of Law, Computer Studies, Health Sciences, Media & Communication, Humanities & Social Sciences, Engineering and Architecture & Design.

Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Nagpur was established in 2021 as an engineering institute. Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Nagpur is a constituent college of Symbiosis International

University, Pune. The institute offers a B. Tech Computer Science and Engineering programme.
